1. Personal Information We Collect

"Personal Information" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. We may collect the following categories of Personal Information:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Registration and Account Information: When you register for our Services, we collect information such as your name (optional), username (must be unique), email address, and password (stored securely hashed). If you registered using an invitation code, we may also record your connection to the user who invited you.
  • Profile Information: Optional information you may add to your account, such as a profile picture, biography, or other identifiers. When you upload a profile picture, we store it and may display it to other users (e.g., your team members).
  • Communication Information: Information you provide when you contact us for customer support, feedback, or other inquiries (e.g., your name, email address, message content).
  • User Content: Content you create, post, or share within the Application (via features that may be added in the future, such as marketplace listings or comments).

Information Collected Automatically (Usage and Device Data):

  • Mining and Activity Data: Times you start and end your mining sessions, session durations, amount of ON Coins earned, your active/inactive status (is_active), your referral code, the identities and activity status of your team members, the number of ads you've watched (adsWatchedToday), ad watch timestamps (lastAdWatchTimestamp), your active boost status (is_on_starter_boost, adBoostExpiryTime).

  • Device and Connection Information: Your device type, model, operating system (and version), hardware settings, unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, Android Advertising ID), IP address, time zone, language preferences, mobile carrier information, signal strength, browser type (if applicable), Application version.
  • Approximate Location Information: Your approximate geographic location (e.g., country, city) derived from your device's IP address or other network information. This may be used to localize our services, generate regional statistics, or comply with legal requirements. We do not collect precise location information (like GPS).
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, local storage, and similar technologies to provide, analyze, and improve our Services. These technologies help us remember your preferences, understand usage trends, and measure the performance of our services.

Information Collected from Third Parties:

  • Advertising Partners: We work with third-party advertising networks, such as Google AdMob, to display ads within the Application and provide you with rewards (e.g., mining speed boosts) for watching them. These partners may collect information about your device identifiers (like Advertising ID), IP address, approximate location, and in-app interactions to serve personalized ads. Their data collection and use practices are governed by their own privacy policies.
  • Analytics Providers: We may use third-party analytics tools, like Firebase Analytics, to help us understand application usage and improve our services. These tools may collect usage data and device information.
  • Other Sources: To the extent permitted by law, we may receive additional information about you from public sources or business partners.
